What's the problem
with philanthropy?


1.

It's not engaging.

Following nonprofits is neither social nor fun.

2.

It's not trustworthy.

It's hard to know when a nonprofit's site is safe. It's your money and time and you want it to be well spent.

3.

It's not visible.

Nonprofits get lost in the mess of social media news feeds, they don't have a specialized space designed to communicate their impact and need.
